A Look Back: The Cyber-Funk DJ Night

Posted on July 21, 2025 by Cygnus

The energy was electric, the beats were heavy, and the neon lights never shone brighter. Our Cyber-Funk DJ Night was a massive success, thanks to our resident DJ, VoidRunner, and everyone who came to dance the night away.

From the moment the first synthwave track dropped, the dance floor was packed. It was incredible to see so many familiar and new faces enjoying the atmosphere we strive to create here at Libra's Club.

We also want to give a special shout-out to our Head Bartender, Jynx, who crafted some exclusive themed drinks for the night. The "Glitch Fuel" was a particular favorite!

Thank you all for making this night unforgettable. We can't wait to see you at our next event, the "Retro Arcade Karaoke" on July 26th. Get your singing voices ready!
